about me

I grew up in a suburb of Worcester Massachusetts, about an hour west of Boston. I always had a driving passion for Music, as well as an infinite curiosity for technology, and how things worked. I spent much of High School running between Concert Band, Marching Band, Chorus, and Drama rehearsals, as well as tinkering with computers on the side in my free time.

This collision of arts and science led me to complete my Bachelors of Music in Sound Recording Technology from the University of Massachusetts at Lowell.

After graduating from UMass, I spent a year freelancing as an audio engineer in the recording studio and live event production, before starting in the pursuit of my Master's degree in Computer Science through the ALIGN Program at Northeastern University.
